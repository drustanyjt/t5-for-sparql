Internationalized Resource Identifier. Equivalent to names

### Gold Entity/Relations

TOVERIFY: Similar to serialization of schema, but with a focus on _subgraphs_ that contain
the necessary relation and entity names.


### Classes, data property, object relation

TOVERIFY: Classes are types of objects, data properties are literals (literals have no outgoing edges), object relations are other entities.
